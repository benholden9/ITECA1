Bookings and Rates

BOOKINGS

Please contact us to check availability and to book our cottage. Alternatively, you can use our Air BnB listing to book your stay, see reviews and find suitable available dates.

RATES

  • The cottage booked for two people - one room: R 950 per night
  • The cottage booked for four people - two rooms: R 1 450 per night
  • Long term booking discounts - longer than a week: 15% discount
  • Monthly: 32% discount

50% deposit payable to confirm the booking. Full payment for the whole booking is due just prior or upon arrival.

Deposit is refundable if cancelled 7 days before booking.

Cancellation Policy

Strict Cancellation Policy Applies as follows: Full refund for cancellations up to 30 days before check-in. If booked fewer than 30 days before check-in, full refund for cancellations made within 48 hours of booking and at least 14 days before check-in. After that, 50% refund up to 7 days before check-in. No refund after that.
