Welcome!

Woodsong Cottage is a spacious, private, self-catering cottage set in a woodland garden of a 44-acre property in the Dargle Valley of the Midlands, KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. The Dargle Valley is a conservancy and the region is often referred to as the “green heart of the KZN Midlands”.

Our self-catering cottage is ideally suited for a couple looking for a private retreat who enjoy being close to nature. It is a perfect base from which to explore the Midlands Meander and it is only 20 minutes from Howick. The cottage is also ideal if you are attending a wedding at one of the 150 Midlands wedding venues nearby.
